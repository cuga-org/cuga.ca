Canadian Underwater Games Association

Join CUGA

CUGA is the national sport organization for underwater hockey, underwater rugby and underwater football in Canada. Membership runs from September 1st to August 31st annually.

Membership Benefits

  • Voting rights at the CUGA Annual General Meeting (AGM) for members 18+.
  • Eligibility to participate in CUGA sanctioned events, including National Championships.
  • Access to National Team selection camps and the ability to represent Canada internationally (subject to selection).
  • Coverage under CUGA's general liability insurance policy during sanctioned activities.
  • Support for club development and access to resources.
  • Regular updates and newsletters about underwater sports in Canada.

Membership Fees

Fees are collected annually and help support the operational costs of CUGA, development programs, and national team support.

  • Adult Membership: $50 CAD
  • Junior Membership (Under 18): $25 CAD

Payment can be made via PayPal. Please ensure you select the correct membership type.

How to Join

  1. Complete Waivers: All members must complete the necessary waivers.
    • Adult Waiver (18+)
    • Minor Waiver (Under 18) - Must be signed by a parent/guardian.

    Please download, complete, and email the signed waiver to info@cuga.org.

  2. Pay Membership Fee: Use the PayPal button above to pay your annual membership fee. Ensure your name in the payment matches the name on your waiver.
  3. Confirmation: Once your waiver and payment are received and processed, you will receive a confirmation email and be added to the membership list.
